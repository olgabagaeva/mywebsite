Olga Bagaeva

Fashion Designer

Fashion designer with Russian-Ukrainian origins, Olga Bagaeva, studied fashion design, pattern making, and stylistics in Liege, Belgium. With a blend of oriental influences intertwined with her occidental education, Olga's designs embody a unique fusion of Eastern and Western aesthetics. Her journey has taken her through various fashion shows, projects, and contests, where she has honed her craft and garnered invaluable experience. Beyond the mere creation of garments, Olga's passion for colors and textures is an art form in itself, allowing her to skillfully transmit female beauty through every facet of her creations. Each piece tells a story, celebrating diversity and individuality while accentuating the timeless allure of femininity.
